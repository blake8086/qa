Buyout auction is an auction

with a set price (the 'buyout' price) that any bidder can accept at any time during the auction, thereby immediately ending the auction and winning the item.[19] If no bidder chooses to utilize the buyout option before the end of bidding the highest bidder wins and pays their bid.[19] Buyout options can be either temporary or permanent.[19] In a temporary buyout auction the option to buy out the auction is no longer available after the first bid is placed.[19] In a permanent buyout auction the buyout option remains available throughout the entire auction until the close of bidding.[19] The buyout price can either remain the same throughout the entire auction, or vary throughout according to preset rules or simply at the whim of the seller.[19]
